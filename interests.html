Interests and Hobbies

Music

Grunge Rock

It's no secret, I've grown up as a grunge-baby most my life. Since I was born at the start of the 90's I spent a majority of my time growing up listening to grunge bands, and it's helped define my taste towards music as a whole cause I'm always waiting to see if a song has that wonderful moment where it just blasts your ears and feels like a kick to the face. Some defining songs for me are:

"What If"
Band: Creed
Album: Human Clay

This song has long been one of my all time favorite songs. The delivery towards the start is the definition of what I love to hear when the heavy part of a song blows in and starts wrecking your shit.

"Dead and Bloated"
Band: Stone Temple Pilots
Album: Core

Another title that jumps out and punches you right in the jaw within the first 20 seconds. There's just something about the vocal delivery combined with the crunch on the guitar which makes me sing along like an idiot in the car whenever this title comes on.
